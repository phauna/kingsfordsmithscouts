What do the leaders' names mean?

Roo - A kangaroo is a Joey's mother, and Roo is short for kangaroo.

Blinky - Blinky Bill is a very famous koala, perhaps you've heard of him?

Boo-boo - Boo-Boo is Yogi Bear's friend who tries to keep him out of trouble.

Bagheera - is a black panther from the Jungle Book.

Jeebi - is a ghost, so watch out!!

Rama - is a buffalo from the Jungle Book.
