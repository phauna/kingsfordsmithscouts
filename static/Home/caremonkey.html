Care Monkey

CareMonkey is a parent-controlled, secure Electronic Health Form (EHF) for organisations with a duty of care such as Scouts. It is being rolled out to all Scout Groups in NSW. When your child is enrolled into our Group an email will be sent to you to invite you to create a profile and fill in medical and other details about the new member/s.

Leaders will issue Activity Permission Forms (formerly E1s) through Care Monkey, which will be sent to parents via email. Parents can easily give permission for activities through email or the Care Monkey app. Care Monkey allows leaders to quickly and easily access medical end contact details for all Group members in case of emergency.
